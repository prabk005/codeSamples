KiiRPA

Kurriyan involved in Raising Punjabi Awareness

The Kurriyan Involved in raising Punjabi awareness (KiiRPA) is a women only non-profit organization which aims to foster a greater understanding of Punjabi tradition, heritage, and culture by educating the youth while providing a social outlet for women to showcase their talent and creativity through a variety of cultural programs and events. The essential purpose of KiiRPA will be to promote Punjabi culture with a strong focus on encouraging women to network, build a support system for one another, and become strong pillars in the community. It is intended to be a non-political, non-religious, non-communal, and non-racial organization.
